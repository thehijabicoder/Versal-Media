Versal-Media Marketing Concepts

Digi Future is an advertising agency that promotes brands with Lift Digital Advertising. The reach of Lift advertising has expanded in tandem with the growth of apartments, malls, and other business areas. People began to choose apartments and flats over single-family residences. The inhabitants utilize a lift roughly four times every day. They frequently converse with other occupants, check their phones for messages, and, most importantly, read whatever information they find within the elevator. That is why Lift advertising has become a must for businesses. In lift advertising, the advertisement image is stored in the projection device and projected on the lift's interior wall. When the lift door is closed, the projection screen begins to screen advertisements and stops automatically when it i.s opened. This allows brands to reach a large audience and capture their interest quickly.
